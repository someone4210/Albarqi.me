Mousa Albarqi is a clinical research leader who has spent over 12 years building one of the most complete careers in Saudi Arabia's CRO landscape — not by moving between organizations, but by mastering every layer of the discipline within one.

From his first days coordinating trials at some of the Kingdom's most respected research institutions, to leading teams across complex multi-site studies, to now holding country-level accountability at MCT-CRO — his trajectory reflects something rare: depth and breadth at the same time.

Today, as Country Lead, Mousa operates at the intersection of science, strategy, and execution. He translates sponsor ambitions into compliant, field-ready operations. He navigates SFDA regulatory frameworks with the confidence of someone who has lived them — not just studied them. And he builds the kind of trust with sites, sponsors, and teams that turns capability into competitive advantage.

Certified as a Project Management Professional (PMP®) and trained through the McKinsey Forward Program, Mousa brings a leadership philosophy that is as rigorous as his clinical background — evidence-based, people-centered, and always oriented toward long-term impact.

He is not building a career in Saudi clinical research. He is helping define what it looks like.
